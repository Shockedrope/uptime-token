// How it works

Time has value.
Now it has a token.

Uptime is a simple way to trade time and services with friends and family. Set your own exchange rate, send tokens freely, and settle up on your terms.

01 —

Add to MetaMask

Import the $UPTI token using the contract address below. Make sure you're on the Polygon network.

02 —

Set your rate

Decide what UPTI is worth to your group. 100 UPTI per hour? 50 per errand? You set the rules.

03 —

Send and receive

Transfer UPTI directly to anyone's wallet. No middlemen, no fees worth mentioning, near-instant settlement.

04 —

Redeem services

Spend your UPTI on whatever you've agreed on. Lawn mowing, babysitting, tech support — the economy is yours.
